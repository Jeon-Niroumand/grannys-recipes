Khoresh Gheymeh Bademjan

Persian Tomato-Eggplant Stew with Lamb and Split Peas

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. lamb leg or shoulder
  • 2 large eggplants
  • 12 vine cherry tomatoes
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3/8 cup yellow split pea (mesured dry)
  • 2 cups or more of water
  • 1 small can tomato sauce
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp salt or more to taste

Preparation and Cooking:

  1. Skin the eggplants with a potato peeler and quarter, halve if small.
  2. Salt the eggplant generously and let them brine for 15 minutes to draw out moisture.
  3. Cut the meat into 1" cubes and sauté the in 3 tbsp oil until golden brown all around.
  4. Finely chop onion and garlic then sauté the onions in same sauce the meat was cooked. After 5 min add the garlic and sauté a few more min.
  5. Add tomato sauce, onion, garlic, meat ,turmeric, black pepper and salt to taste to a pot with 2 cups boiled water and let simmer on low to medium heat for 1 hr.
  6. Cook the split peas in boiling water until al dente (20-45 min. depending on brand).Drain them, rinse and set aside.
  7. Fry the eggplant slices until golden brown with remaining olive oil.
  8. After 1 hr. of letting the stew simmer, add more water if necessary and add the fried eggplants. Make sure they are fully submerged. Lay the tomatoes on top and cover with the lid. Let the stew simmer for another 30 min. over low to medium heat.
  9. After 15 min. add the split peas and let it all simmer together.
  10. Serve with saffroned Basmati rice.
