Granny's Lil' Smokies

A Simple Crockpot Lil' Smokies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 jar of apple jelly
  • 1 bottle of Heinz Spicy Ketchup
  • 1 package of all beef lil' smokies

Preparation and Cooking:

  1. Mix apple jelly, spicy ketchup and lil' smokies into the crockpot. Tobasco or your favorite hotsauce may be substitued with regular ketchup, spice to taste.
  2. Keep crokcpot on low or medium heat and cook for 2 hrs. Mix ocassionally.
  3. Serve with toothpicks on small paper plates.
